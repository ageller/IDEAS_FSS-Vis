The Physical Evolution of a Calcium-rich Supernova

Here we present the evolution of calcium-rich supernova (SN) 2019ehk discovered in host galaxy Messier 100. In the top left plot, we visualize SN 2019ehk as a circle whose radius is expanding in time, while the SN temperature rapidly decreases as shown by the colorbar. The top right plot shows telescope imaging of SN 2019ehk at different phases after explosion. The bottom panel shows the luminosity, radius and temperature of SN 2019ehk as the SN evolves.
