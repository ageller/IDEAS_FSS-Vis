Visualization of Vortex Matter

3D Interactive Visualization of Vortices in Artificial Pinning site

Abdulwahab Al Luhaibi

In type -II superconductors, vortices penetrate the system in quantized form of fluxoid, φ0, when the applied magnetic field exceed the first critical field. Here, we provide the results of simulating a system with a spherical pinning site in the middle of the material using Time Dependent Ginzburg Landau Equation (TDGL). The result is normalized order parameter, |ψ|2, which can be mapped spatially that normalized by the coherence length, ξ0. The three interactive figures are shown |ψ|2 at zero magnetic field, φ0 per area unit cell magnetic field, and at current density, J, exceeding the critical current density, Jc, at the time when vortices depin .
